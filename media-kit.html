About

The Perspective Behind the Work

I'm an architect and interior designer specialising in refined, atmospheric spaces that balance materiality, light, and function. My work creates environments for clients that feel both intentional and emotionally engaging — real spaces that tell a story.

Through @haus_momster, I share my design process, ongoing projects, and the aesthetic world I inhabit daily with a community of design lovers, homeowners, and industry professionals.

My content is rooted in authenticity — I only showcase products, materials, and brands that genuinely integrate into how I design, live, and create. Every collaboration is concept-led, not product-led.

Designed Spaces

Licensed architect working on residential projects — content is drawn from real client work and personal spaces.

Editorial Aesthetic

Atmospheric, carefully styled imagery with attention to light, detail, and material quality.

Authentic Storytelling

High engagement driven by genuine lifestyle integration, not staged product placement.

German Market Reach

Majority DACH audience — ideal for brands entering or deepening their presence in the German-speaking market.
